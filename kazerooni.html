Project Overview

Kazerooni is a Dubai-based property management portal that digitizes and automates the entire property lifecycle. The platform serves three distinct user groups: tenants (seeking properties and managing leases), landlords (tracking income and property performance), and admin teams (managing maintenance, payments, and compliance). The goal was to create an intuitive, Arabic-friendly interface that handles complex real estate workflows while maintaining exceptional user experience.

Key Features

Advanced Property Search

Filter properties by location, price, size, amenities, and availability with map integration and saved searches.

Digital Lease Management

End-to-end lease creation, e-signatures, renewal reminders, and document storage with compliance tracking.

Maintenance Requests

Tenants can submit maintenance requests with photos; admin assigns vendors; real-time status updates.

Financial Dashboard

Rent collection tracking, expense management, profit/loss statements, and tax reporting for landlords.

Automated Notifications

Email and SMS reminders for rent due dates, maintenance updates, and document expiry.

Bilingual Support

Full Arabic and English interface with RTL layout support for Arabic users.

Design Approach

User-Centered Design: Conducted interviews with Dubai-based property managers and tenants to understand local market nuances. Created distinct user flows for each persona.

RTL & Localization: Designed with RTL in mind from the start, ensuring seamless Arabic experience. Used appropriate cultural imagery and color psychology.

Complex Data Simplified: Transformed complex property data into digestible visualizations and progressive disclosure patterns to avoid overwhelming users.
