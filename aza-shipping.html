Project Overview

Aza Shipping Pvt. Ltd., a Dubai-based shipping and logistics company, needed a modern intranet portal to replace their outdated communication methods. Employees were struggling with scattered documents, inefficient task tracking, and poor internal communication. The goal was to create a centralized platform that would streamline daily operations and foster better collaboration across departments.

I collaborated with a cross-functional team to design 25+ responsive web screens, including dashboards, user profiles, document centers, and task management modules. The design focused on simplicity and efficiency, ensuring that employees could quickly access the information and tools they needed.

Key Features

Personal Dashboard

Customizable dashboard showing pending tasks, recent documents, announcements, and calendar events at a glance.

Document Management

Centralized document repository with version control, advanced search, and role-based access permissions.

Task & Project Tracking

Create, assign, and track tasks with deadlines, priorities, and status updates. Visual progress indicators.

Employee Directory

Searchable directory with profiles, contact information, departments, and organizational charts.

Company Calendar

Shared calendar for holidays, events, meetings, and deadlines with integration with task management.

Announcements

Company-wide announcements with targeting options, read receipts, and archive functionality.
